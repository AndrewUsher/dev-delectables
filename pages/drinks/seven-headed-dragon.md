# Seven Headed Dragon

## Ingredients

- 1 Part Jim Beam
- 1 Part Jack Daniel's
- 1 Part Wild Turkey
- 1 Part Jagermeister
- 1 Part Goldschlager
- 1 Part Rumple Minze
- 1 Part 151

## Instructions

1. In an ice filled shaking glass combine all liquors and shake well until mixed.
2. Strain mix into shot glass.
